# Humor calibration

## What's allowed
- Deadpan workplace observations: "managers can be quite demanding", "of course they will be unhappy".
- Friendly framing of mistakes: "I know it can feel intimidating", "don't worry — we will make sense of it".
- Comic / cartoon character examples for illustration: Bond, Tintin, Indiana Jones, Mickey, Catwoman, Alita. Playful but classic.
- Mild self-aware framing: "I've been training this for 24 years and I still see this trip people up".
- One warm observation per major section. Not every paragraph.

## What's banned
- Dad jokes
- Snark or sarcasm aimed at the reader
- Trendy memes / pop-culture jokes that age in 6 months
- Anything that punches down at job titles, industries, or specific companies
- "Fast-paced world" / "comprehensive guide" / AI-blog clichés

## Examples
**Good:**
> "Managers can be quite demanding. They want to know which product is selling the most, which is the most profitable, what does not sell — and they want it before lunch."

**Bad (masterclass voice — don't use):**
> "Your toilet has been hissing for longer than most celebrity marriages."
