# Do-not-write list

If any of these appear in a draft, the voice-pass or seo-pass stage MUST flag and rewrite.

## AI tells
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "In an era where..."
- "It's no secret that..."
- "Whether you're a [X] or a [Y]..."
- "Let's dive in" / "let's dive deep"
- "In this comprehensive guide..."
- "navigate the complexities of"
- "unlock your potential"
- "harness the power of"
- "delve into"
- "leverage" (as a verb, when "use" works)
- "robust" (when "strong" or "solid" works)
- "moreover", "furthermore", "additionally" (start sentences with concrete content instead)
- "in conclusion", "in summary"
- "the world of [X]"
- em-dash chains (— — ) as a stylistic tic

## Voice mismatches for Vinai
- Dad jokes
- Edgy / snarky lines
- "Fast-paced", "ever-changing", "dynamic landscape"
- Anything punching down at industries, roles, or named companies
- Generic motivational phrasing without a concrete next-action
